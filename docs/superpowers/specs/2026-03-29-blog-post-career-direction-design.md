## Structure

### 1. Where I am now

Quality Team Leader at Scott & Fyfe, building something worthwhile, but aware this is a step on a longer journey rather than the destination.

### 2. Where I want to go

Quality Manager. Not just a title but the ability to shape quality culture at a higher level, to influence how organisations approach quality from the top down.

### 3. The roadmap

Each qualification is deliberate, not box-ticking:

- HNC Mechanical Engineering (in progress) - the technical foundation
- Lead Auditor - understanding systems from the outside in
- ASQ Certified Quality Manager - recognised professional standard
- ASQ Quality Engineer - deepening the technical quality knowledge
- CQI Chartered status - the professional benchmark in the UK

### 4. Why it matters

The motivational core of the post. Quality done well changes how organisations work. It is not just about catching defects - it is about building systems and cultures where people can do their best work and customers get what they expect. That is worth committing to fully.

### 5. Closing

Honest that the path is long and the work is ongoing. But the direction is set and the reasons are clear.
